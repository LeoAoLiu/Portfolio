Backdrop is a House designed for a hypothetical theater painter. The painter in mind enjoys painting locations and settings throughout the day, but also has a side interest of theater design. A few times per month, he hosts theater events where he welcomes the public as well as theater groups to get together.

The design of this house focuses on the existing surroundings of Giardini di via Carlo Felice, which naturally has different types of public spaces on either side. The design is a simple linear space that “patches” the part of the Aurelian wall that has fallen. The minimal approach of the design allows the building to have multiple uses, allowing the artist to post his work as he is working on them. The cantilever of the studio also provides enhancements to the surrounding public spaces without actually contacting them, providing shade as well as potential garden and theater spaces.
