Slit is an experimental housing complex designed by combining multiple unit types through a matt typology. The exercise started off by choosing 3 distinct families within Literature—which can be from books, movies, or TV shows—and designing a house for each family based on a created scenario. Once the units are designed, they are placed onto a site through various methods to combine and merge them into something more comprehensive.
