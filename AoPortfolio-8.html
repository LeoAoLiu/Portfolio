Tool Evaluation: Nuit Blance Cushion

After testing the agents within various spatial conditions and establishing their

limits, we can evaluate this simulation tool by first simulating architectural spaces at the smallest of scales. Cushion is a Nuit Blanche Toronto installation space that invites people to walk through a narrow corridor, where the light filled ballons change color as people interact with them.
